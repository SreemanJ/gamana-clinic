About Dr. Manaswi Reddy

Where Medical Science Meets Movement

I am Dr. Manaswi Reddy, founder of Gamana Clinic. With an MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery) and a Master's in Exercise and Sports Science, I offer a unique dual perspective that bridges traditional medicine with therapeutic exercise interventions.

"Gamana" represents the journey toward optimal health through movement. My approach combines clinical medical knowledge with evidence-based exercise physiology, yoga therapy, and sports science to treat chronic diseases, enhance athletic performance, and prevent lifestyle-related conditions.

Unlike conventional fitness training, my prescriptions are rooted in medical understanding of pathophysiology, ensuring safe and effective interventions for complex health conditions including diabetes, cardiovascular disease, metabolic disorders, and musculoskeletal issues.

Clinical Approach

The Gamana Method:
Medical Precision + Human Movement

1

Medical Assessment

Comprehensive health screening, medical history review, and clinical risk stratification using my MBBS training to identify contraindications and safe exercise parameters.

2

Physiological Testing

Exercise stress testing, body composition analysis, cardiovascular fitness assessment, and movement screening to establish baseline data.

3

Integrated Prescription

FITT-VP principles (Frequency, Intensity, Time, Type, Volume, Progression) combined with yoga therapy and lifestyle medicine for holistic treatment.

4

Monitored Progression

Regular reassessment of clinical markers (BP, glucose, lipids, fitness parameters) with program adjustments based on physiological response.
